Banku

Description

Banku is a staple Ghanaian dish made from fermented corn and cassava dough. It is usually served with soup, stew or grilled fish and pepper.

This recipe is popular across Ghana and is a comfort food for many families.

Ingredients.

  • 2 cups of corn dough.
  • 1 cup cassava dough.
  • 3 cups of water.
  • Salt to taste.

Steps

  1. Mix corn dough mixture and stir continously.
  2. Cook for 20-30 mins untill smooth and stretchy.
  3. Serve hot with soup or stew.
