Jallof

Description

Ghanaian Jallof Rice is a flavorful one-pot dish made with rice, tomtoes, and spices. It's smoky, slightly spicy and a party favorite across west Africa.

Ingredients.

  • 3 cups of long grain rice.
  • 1 can of tomatoes or 5 fresh tomatoes blended.
  • 3 table spoons of tomato paste.
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil.
  • 2 teaspoons of curry powder.
  • 3 cups of chicken stock.
  • Salt and Maggi to taste.

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot and fry the onions until golden.
  2. Add tomato paste and fry for 3 mins.
  3. Add blended tomatoes, cur.y, thyme, salt and pepper. Cook until the oil floats to the top.
  4. Pour in chicken stock and bring it to a boil.
  5. Wash the rice and add it to te pot. Stir once.
  6. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 30-35 mins.
  7. Food is readyyy!
